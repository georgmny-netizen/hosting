About Company
Yongyeokyo Trading House Co., Ltd. is an international trading company headquartered in South Korea. The company is a co-founder of a production enterprise in Malaysia located within the East Coast Economic Region (ECER).
Through its production base and strategic logistics positioning, the company operates as a regional APAC supply hub supporting controlled delivery routes into South Korea, Japan, Taiwan and China.
