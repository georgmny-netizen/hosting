Legal Framework for Controlled Materials

This framework applies to YONGYEOKYO high-purity metals, rare earth materials, Korea-origin or Korea-routed APAC shipments and controlled RFQ scenarios. Commercial activity is governed by compliance review, technical validation and batch-specific certification.

Export-Control Disclaimer

Certain products may be subject to export control regulations depending on material specification, purity level, destination and end-use. Supply may require verification of end-user, application and shipment routing prior to approval.

No-Public-Offer Disclaimer

This website and its contents do not constitute a legally binding public offer. All transactions are subject to formal quotation, mutual agreement and executed contract documentation.

RFQ Subject-to-Specification Disclaimer

All RFQs are subject to technical validation, specification alignment, GDMS/ICP-MS route confirmation, CoA release, supplier confirmation and APAC compliance review.

Controlled Materials Notice

Certain rare earth elements, isotopically enriched materials and ultra-high purity products may require additional documentation, regulatory approval and compliance review prior to release.

Batch & Certification Disclaimer
